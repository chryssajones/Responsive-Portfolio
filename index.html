About Me

Hi! My name is Chryssa Jones and I'm an aspiring full stack developer, enrolled in the Full Stack Developer Boot Camp at UC San Diego. I'm currently working as a higher education administrator at UC San Diego, and I have 8 years of experience in this industry. Previously, I've worked as an operations manager, a project manager, a marketing and social media director, a copy writer and editor, a bookkeeper, and a small-business owner.

I love coding because it gives me an opportunity to be creative and precise, and seeing my creations unfold exactly as I want them to is really exciting! Finding and squashing bugs is a fun part of the process, like a scavenger hunt. I love the flexibility it gives me, I love being part of a team, and I'm looking forward to a new career opportunity.
